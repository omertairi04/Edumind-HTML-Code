Welcome to Kumanova

The heart of northeastern North Macedonia

Famous Places to Visit

Kokino Observatory

Explore one of the world's oldest observatories, dating back over 4,000 years. Learn about ancient stargazing techniques and enjoy breathtaking views.

The Church of St. George

A stunning medieval church in Staro Nagoričane, featuring incredible frescoes from the 14th century.

Eski Mosque

This historic mosque showcases beautiful Ottoman architecture and cultural significance.

Local Food

  • Tavče Gravče: A hearty bean stew baked to perfection.
  • Ajvar: A delicious roasted red pepper spread, a Macedonian favorite.
  • Kumanovo Sausage: Spicy and smoky, a must-try for meat lovers.

Fun Facts About Kumanovo

  • Kumanovo is named after the Kuman tribe, a Turkic nomadic people.
  • The Kokino Observatory was ranked the fourth oldest in the world by NASA.
  • Kumanovo hosts a vibrant Carnival every February with colorful costumes and parades.
