This is a burger

Ingredients

  • Bun (2 halves)
  • Beef patty (1, cooked)
  • Cheddar cheese (1 slice)
  • Lettuce leaves (2)
  • Tomato slices (2)
  • Onion rings (optional, 3 slices)
  • Pickles (optional, 2 slices)
  • Mayonnaise (1 tbsp)
  • Ketchup (1 tbsp)
  • Mustard (optional, 1 tsp)

Instructions

  • Prepare the ingredients:
    1. Cook the beef patty on a skillet or grill until done to your liking.
    2. Slice the tomato, onion, and pickles (if using).
    3. Toast the burger buns lightly for added texture.
  • Assemble the burger:
    1. Spread mayonnaise on the bottom half of the bun.
    2. Place a lettuce leaf on top of the mayonnaise.
    3. Add the cooked beef patty and a slice of cheddar cheese.
    4. Top with tomato slices, onion rings, and pickles.
    5. Spread ketchup and mustard (if desired) on the top half of the bun.
  • Serve:
    1. Place the top half of the bun onto the assembled ingredients.
    2. Serve the burger immediately, accompanied by fries or your preferred side dish.
